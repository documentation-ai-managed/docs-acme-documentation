## Troubleshooting Site Access

Follow these steps to resolve common issues.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Your Connection" icon="wifi">
    Verify internet speed >5Mbps. Test other sites.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear Browser Cache" icon="trash-2">
    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="Chrome" icon="chrome">
        Press `Ctrl+Shift+Delete`, select "Cached images and files", clear data.
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="Firefox" icon="firefox">
        Go to Settings > Privacy > Cookies and Site Data > Clear Data.
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Disable Extensions" icon="plug">
    Turn off ad blockers or VPNs temporarily. Reload the page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try Incognito Mode" icon="incognito">
    Open a private window. If it works, an extension causes the issue.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="alert">
  Persistent problems? Contact support with your browser version and error screenshot.
</Callout>
